Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel or modify my booking?

You can cancel or modify your booking up to 2 hours before the showtime through your account dashboard or by contacting our support team.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and mobile payment methods like Apple Pay and Google Pay.

How do I receive my tickets?

After successful payment, you'll receive digital tickets via email and SMS. You can also access them through your account on our website or app.

Is there a booking fee?

We charge a small convenience fee per transaction to maintain our platform and provide excellent service. The fee is clearly displayed before payment.

Can I choose my seats?

Yes! Our interactive seat map allows you to choose your preferred seats for all participating cinemas.

Do you offer group discounts?

We offer special rates for group bookings of 10 or more tickets. Contact our support team for more information about group discounts.
